# Shibby v0.0.2 Commands
## Audio/Video
- **!audio** *url*
 - Grabs video/audio from URL and reuploads as a .mp3
- **!video** *url*
 - Grabs video from URL and reuploads as a .mp4
## Random Numbers
- **!roll** *x*d*y*
 - Rolls an x number of y sided dice; e.g. !roll 1d20
- **!flip**
 - Flips a coin; heads or tails!
## Fun
- **!hello**
 - Hi!
- **!goodbot**
 - Praise the bot!
- **!badbot**
 - Scold the bot!
